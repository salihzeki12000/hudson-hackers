About

The Hudson Hackers were established in 2013. They were brought together by their mutual desire to plan itineraries and love of JavaScript. PlanIt became their first major project, which they sold to FourSquare for $1000. Tragically, the Hackers disappeared from the public eye after the sale of PlanIt. However, locals say you can still hear the sound of their keyboards clicking on late nights in East Campus.

How to Use PlanIt

PlanIt is the best way to make the most of the special moments in life, namely events that require extra planning: road trips, anniversaries, visits from relatives, and more. This app allows users to organize locations into editable itineraries.
